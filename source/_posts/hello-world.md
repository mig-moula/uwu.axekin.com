# Project Philosophy and Intent

It can be difficult to understand, but this **project** is entirely separate from **my website**. I do not want them to be associated in any way.

The reason is simple: I always stay true to my principles, especially my commitment to **never ask for money**. My stance has been the same on **[Nebulo](https://nebulo.click/about)**, on my **Discord server**, and now here.  
If I were doing this for profit, it would have been obvious from the very beginning.

# Archiving as a Core Value

Regarding torrent or FTP accessibility, I provide these options because **archiving** is my top priority—an essential part of my ideals, principles, and the purpose of my website.  
That is why I built things this way.

I am currently subscribed to a hosting plan that I can afford. As long as I can pay for it, the service will continue to exist.

# Sustainability and Donations

However, I am not going to ruin myself just to offer everything for free. I have limits.

If you want more data and more bandwidth, you can simply **[make a donation](https://ankergames.net/donations)**.  
(__If you make a donation to this site/project, please note this in the donation comments section or wherever you can__)
Once donations become stable, I will start increasing capacity. I will not constantly scale the server up and down depending on fluctuating contributions.

This is a **give-and-take** system:

- **Those who want to donate** may do so.  
- **Those who don’t** can still enjoy the service freely.

The service will not disappear if donations stop; donations are simply an added bonus that helps it grow.
